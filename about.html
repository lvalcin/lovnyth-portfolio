About Me

My journey into technology started in healthcare, where I have worked as a medical technologist for over 7 years now. That experience helped me build strong attention to detail, problem-solving skills, and the ability to work in fast-paced environments where accuracy matters.

While I enjoyed my work in healthcare, I found myself increasingly drawn to the world of technology and programming. Over time, I became more curious about how systems and software work behind the scenes. That curiosity led me to explore programming and eventually transition into tech.

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Applied Science in Information Technology at Broward College with a focus on web development. I enjoy learning new technologies and building projects using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python.

My goal is to grow into full stack developement and build meaningful applications that solve real problems.
