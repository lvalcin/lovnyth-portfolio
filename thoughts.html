My Experience Building This Website

Building this website was a really good learning experience for me. It gave me the chance to actually apply what I’ve been learning instead of just reading or watching tutorials. Working on multiple pages and trying to keep everything consistent helped me understand how websites are structured and how different parts connect.

Challenges I Faced

One of the main challenges I ran into was getting my styling to look the way I wanted across all pages. Sometimes I would fix something on one page and it would affect another, which was frustrating at first. Over time, I started to understand how CSS works more and how to organize my code better.

How This Helps My Career

As someone transitioning from healthcare into the tech field, this project is an important step for me. It shows that I am not only learning but also applying those skills in a real project. I am currently continuing my education in Information Technology, and projects like this help build the foundation I need for a future career in software and web development.

Final Thoughts

Overall, this project helped me feel more confident in my ability to build something from scratch. I know I still have a lot to learn, but this experience made me more excited to keep going, continue my education, and keep improving my skills in tech.
