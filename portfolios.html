The Importance of an Electronic Portfolio

An electronic portfolio is similar to a resume because it presents an individual's skills and experience to potential employers and/or clients. However, a portfolio goes further than a basic resume. An electronic portfolio not only allows an individual to provide professional information, but it also allows them to showcase their work. Keeping this in mind, one should always display their best work. A portfolio can open up new opportunities and may serve as the first impression a potential employer has of an individual.

What My Individual Portfolio Should Include

As I will be seeking a career in software and web development, my portfolio should include actual projects and real work examples that demonstrate my coding knowledge and technical skills. Important elements of my portfolio should include the following:

  • Headline with statement of purpose
  • Services Offered
  • Best Portfolio Work/Projects
  • About Me Section with a summary of experience
  • Contact Information(email, social media, GitHub)
